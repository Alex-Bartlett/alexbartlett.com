What can you bring?

We recommend you bring a sealed plastic water bottle. The weather may be warm and we encourage you to stay hydrated! Our bars will be happy to refill your water bottles for you throughout the day.

No food or drink, including alcohol, is permitted to be brought on site, other than, if deemed appropriate, to accommodate the needs of young children or, in some cases, specific dietary requirement. Age Restrictions

We happily welcome those of all ages to the festival, including families. However, under 16s must be accompanied by a ticket holder over 18 at all times.

Challenge 25: We operate a challenge 25 policy. Please bring ID if you look under 25 years old and remember that legally there is no automatic right to be served alcohol. Service is always at the discretion of the licensee for whatever reason they decide, and the licensee is under no obligation to explain the reason if service is refused.
