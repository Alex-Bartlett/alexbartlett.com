Portfolio

Here is a selection of the projects I have worked on that I'm the most proud of. Click on a project to find out more.

UpSkill Job Management

A CRUD web app that helps you keep track of your on-going jobs, visualise your customers, and manage your staff's site history.

Designed to run on your business' intranet, you can store details about each job you work on, such as daily progress, PO history, and project goals.

Rochford Festival Site

I designed and created the website for the 2023 Rochford Festival. The site is responsive and accessible, with extra work put in to achieve the focus of keeping it as minimal and easy to use as possible.
